                    HOW TO ACCESS THE CONFIG SCREEN
================================================================================

METHOD 1: From Main Menu
-------------------------
1. Launch Minecraft (don't join a server yet)
2. On the main menu, click "MODS" button
3. Scroll through the mod list on the LEFT side
4. Find "Auto Answer Mod" and CLICK on it
5. On the RIGHT side, click the CONFIGURE/SETTINGS button (gear/wrench icon)
   
METHOD 2: From Pause Menu (In-Game)
------------------------------------
1. Press ESC while in-game
2. Click "Mod Menu" or "Mods" button
3. Find "Auto Answer Mod" in the list
4. Click the configure button
